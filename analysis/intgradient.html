Interior gradient estimate

  • Suppose \(u\) is harmonic in \(B_1\), then \[\sup_{B_{\frac{1}{2}}}|\nabla u|\leq c\sup_{\partial B_1}|u|\] where \(c=c(n)>0\).

  • Proof:

    In Euclidean space the calculation of Laplacian is much easier: \[ \Delta |\nabla u|^2 = (u^2_j)_{ii}=(2u_{ij}u_j)_i=2u^2_{ij}+2u_{iij}u_j=2u^2_{ij} \] with a reminder that \(u\) is harmonic. Now \(|Du|^2\) is subharmonic. Choose bump function \(\varphi=\eta^2\in C^1_0(B_1)\) later and we first calculate that \[ \begin{align} \Delta (\eta^2|\nabla u|^2)&=\eta^2 \Delta |\nabla u|^2 + |\nabla u|^2\Delta \eta^2 + 2\nabla \eta^2 \nabla |\nabla u|^2 \\ &= 2\eta^2 u^2_{ij} + u^2_j(2\eta \eta_{ii} + 2\eta_i^2)+4\eta \eta_i 2u_{ji}u_j \\ &= 2u^2_j\eta \eta_{ii} + 2u^2_j\eta^2_i + 8\eta u_{ij}\eta_i u_j + 2\eta^2 u^2_{ij} + 8\eta^2_i u^2_j- 8\eta^2_i u^2_j\\ &=2u^2_j\eta \eta_{ii} + 2u^2_j\eta^2_i + 2(2\eta_iu_j+\eta u_{ij})^2- 8\eta^2_i u^2_j\\ &\geq 2u^2_j\eta \eta_{ii} + 2u^2_j\eta^2_i- 8\eta^2_i u^2_j\\ &= 2u^2_j\eta \eta_{ii}- 6\eta^2_i u^2_j\\ &= (2\eta \Delta \eta-6|\nabla u|^2|\nabla \eta|^2)|\nabla u|^2 \end{align} \] Put \(\eta=1\) in \(B_{\frac{1}{2}}\), then the RHS is bounded below by some \(-C\). By taking \(\alpha\) large enough we obtain \[\Delta (\eta^2|\nabla u|^2 + \alpha u^2)\geq 0.\] By maximum principle, we have \[\eta^2 |\nabla u|^2\leq \eta^2|\nabla u|^2 + \alpha u^2 \leq \sup_{\partial B_1} (\eta^2|\nabla u|^2 + \alpha u^2)=\alpha \sup_{\partial B_1} u^2.\] In \(B_{\frac{1}{2}}\) we have \[\sup_{B_{\frac{1}{2}}}|\nabla u|\leq c \sup_{\partial B_1} |u| \]
