Generalized H\"older inequality

  • Suppose \(\frac{1}{p_1}+\frac{1}{p_2}+\ldots+\frac{1}{p_n}=\frac{1}{r}\) for some positive numbers \(p_1, p_2. \ldots, p_n, r\). Let \(f_1, \ldots, f_n\) be \(L^p\) functions, then we have \[||f_1\ldots f_n||_r \leq ||f_1||_{p_1}\ldots ||f_n||_{p_n}.\]

  • Proof.

  • The inequality can be re-stated as: \[||f_1\ldots f_k||_{\left(\frac{1}{p_1}+\ldots+\frac{1}{p_k}\right)^{-1}} \leq ||f_1||_{p_1}\ldots ||f_k||_{p_k}.\] We consider the induction step, i.e. \(n=k+1\): \[ \begin{align} \int |f_1 \ldots f_k|^r |f_{k+1}|^r &\leq \left( \int |f_1 \ldots f_k|^{ \left(\frac{1}{p_1}+\ldots+\frac{1}{p_k} \right)^{-1}} \right)^{r\left(\frac{1}{p_1}+\ldots+\frac{1}{p_k}\right)} \left( \int |f_{k+1}|^{p_{k+1}}\right)^{\frac{r}{p_{k+1}}}\\ &= ||f_1\ldots f_k||^r_{\left(\frac{1}{p_1}+\ldots+\frac{1}{p_k}\right)^{-1}}||f_{k+1}||^r_{p_k+1}\\ &\leq ||f_1||_{p_1}^r\ldots ||f_k||_{p_k}^r. \end{align} \]
