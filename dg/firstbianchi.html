First Bianchi Identity: the statement

\[R^l_{ijk}+R^l_{jki}+R^l_{kij}=0\]

Proof

In normal coordinates, we have \[R^l_{ijk}=\partial_i \Gamma^l_{jk}-\partial_j \Gamma^l_{ik}.\] The given LHS is equal to \[\partial_i \Gamma^l_{jk}-\partial_j \Gamma^l_{ik}+ \partial_j \Gamma^l_{ki}-\partial_k \Gamma^l_{ji}+ \partial_j \Gamma^l_{ij}-\partial_i \Gamma^l_{kj}=0.\]
