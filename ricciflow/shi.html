  • Shi estimate

  • Let \( (M^n, g(t))\) be a Ricci flow and \(M\) closed. For each \( \alpha>0, m\in \mathbb{N}\) there exists a constant \(C_m=C(m,n,\max\{\alpha, 1\})\) such that if \[|Rm(x,t)|_{g(t)}\leq K\] for all \(t\in [0,\frac{\alpha}{K}]\), then \[|\nabla^m Rm(x,t)|_{g(t)}\leq \frac{C_mK}{t^{m/2}}\] for all \(t\in [0,\frac{\alpha}{K}]\).
